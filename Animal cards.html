Cat

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus,
  • Average Life Span: between 13-17 years,
  • Habitat Coasts, Deserts, Forests, Grasslands,Mountains.

Cats Have Fewer Taste Buds Than Dogs or People. Only 20% of Orange Cats Are Female. Cats make about 100 different sounds. Milk Is Not Good For Cats! Most Cats Are Lactose Intolerant. Cats spend between 30 to 50 percent of their day grooming themselves.
