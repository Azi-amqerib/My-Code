Animal Treading Cards

Cat

Cats Have Fewer Taste Buds Than Dogs or People. Only 20% of Orange Cats Are Female. Cats make about 100 different sounds. Milk Is Not Good For Cats! Most Cats Are Lactose Intolerant. Cats spend between 30 to 50 percent of their day grooming themselves.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus,
  • Average Life Span: between 13-17 years,
  • Habitat: Coasts, Deserts, Forests, Grasslands, Mountains,
  • Diet: Cats are carnivores, which means they eat mostly meat,
  • Behaviour: Play, Communicate, Hunting, Grooming, Rolling Over.

Parrot

Parrots are found in warm climates all over most of the world. Many birds grip food in their feet, but only Parrots can hold their food up to their beaks whilst they eat. The smallest parrot is the buff-faced pygmy parrot, which is only about 3 inches. Many species are monogamous and spend their lives with only one mate. Many parrot species fly miles and miles each day. Parrots are intelligent birds. They have relatively large brains, they can learn, and they can use simple tools.

  • Scientific Name: Psittaciformes,
  • Average Life Span: A parrot’s lifespan is about 15-20 years,
  • Habitat: Most wild parrots live in the warm areas of the Southern Hemisphere,
  • Diet: Parrots primarily eat Fruits, Nuts, Seeds, Flowers,Insects,
  • Behaviour: Beak grinding, beak wiping, chattering loudly, drooping wings, heavy breathing, moulting, muttering softly, plucking.
